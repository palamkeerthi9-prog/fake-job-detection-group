⚠️ How to Spot Fake Jobs

Stay one step ahead of scammers with these red flags.

Requests for Money

Legitimate employers NEVER ask for registration fees, training costs, or money for equipment checks.

Unprofessional Emails

Watch out for emails from free domains (e.g., @gmail.com, @yahoo.com) instead of official company domains.

Instant Hiring

Getting hired instantly without a proper interview or background check is a major warning sign.
